What this proves

  • Operating discipline. A research artefact is a build, not a document. Schemas, fail-fast gates, immutable canonical state, regression rules after each failure.
  • Multi-model orchestration. Strategic models (Claude Opus 4.7) plan and red-team. Three deep-research workers (Perplexity Sonar Deep Research, Alibaba Tongyi DeepResearch 30B, OpenAI o4-mini deep-research) fan out in parallel. Claude Haiku 4.5 swarms structure the data. Python runs every calculation. The right model for the right job, with explicit cost/quality tradeoffs.
  • Evidence over polish. Three rules govern every output: every number has a verified source URL; quotes are ≤15 words verbatim from the source; nulls with documented gaps beat fabricated values.
  • Frontend engineering. Vanilla HTML+CSS, no framework, no build step. Loads under 50 KB gzip. Playwright-audited mobile-first design, sticky navigation on tablet+, bottom-sheet drawer on mobile, dark mode, safe-area insets, print stylesheet.
  • Deployable today. Vercel and GitHub Pages configurations are checked in. Push to main → live in production.
