How to read this pack

  1. Start with the Model Provenance page. Every claim downstream is only as honest as the model that produced it. The provenance page enumerates every model, its billing class (paid vs free), and the artefacts it produced.
  2. Then JD Coverage Map. Maps every Russian-original JD bullet to a STRONG / PARTIAL / GAP verdict and a closure plan. Domain-expertise gap is declared, not hidden.
  3. Competitor Intelligence and Subscription Economics are the two analytical anchors; they are sourced from a 28-peer dataset and Perplexity Sonar deep search.
  4. MVP Loop and 30-60-90 plan translate the analysis into operating commitments — capability-conditional, not promised.
